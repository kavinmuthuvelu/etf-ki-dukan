📋 How This App Works

1. Screener Tab
Browse all 66 ETFs ranked by proximity to their 52-week low. Rank 1 = closest to annual low = buy first.

2. Buying Rule
Buy 1 ETF per day. Start with Rank 1. If already owned, go to Rank 2, and so on. Buy during last 30–60 min of market hours.

3. Averaging
If your ETF falls to a Pi level (−3.14%, −6.28%, etc.), add 1 more part. This lowers your average cost.

4. Profit Exit
Sell when CMP crosses your Avg Cost × (1 + target%). The target chips in Portfolio show your exact exit prices.

5. Note
Prices shown are reference values from the Excel sheet. Use NSE/BSE for live prices before trading.

⚠️ This is not investment advice. Always verify prices on NSE/BSE before executing orders.
